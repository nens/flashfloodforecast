Flash Flood Forecaster

Flash® is the most inclusive flash flood forecast information service for water authorities in Australia and Worldwide

Read more »

Flash® is a user friendly flash flood forecast information service for all levels of government, local residents and businesses who want to protect their critical assets.

Flash® provides the most inclusive flood forecasts at any location within Australia (or Worldwide) at any time. Fast and reliable Flash is Cloud based operating on the ‘Lizard’ platform.
